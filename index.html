About

My work examines how energy affordability, environmental conditions, and policy choices shape household vulnerability. I am especially interested in questions related to energy burden, eviction filing rates, environmental exposure, health outcomes, poverty, and inequality.

My broader goal is to use applied economic research to study how environmental and energy policies affect low-income and vulnerable communities.

Welcome to my website.

I am a PhD student in Resource Economics at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. My research interests are in energy economics, environmental economics, development economics, and applied microeconometrics.

My work examines how energy and environmental conditions shape household wellbeing, especially for vulnerable communities. I am interested in using causal methods to study questions related to energy affordability, housing stability, environmental exposure, and economic inequality.

Outside research, I enjoy building accessible data dashboards that make economic and environmental issues easier to engage with and understand.
